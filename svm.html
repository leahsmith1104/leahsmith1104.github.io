Sky Vegas Millions

Client: Sky Betting and Gaming - Sky Vegas the is the UK's most popular online casino, it's the ultimate destination for new, exclusive, and exciting online slots. They bring the thrill of the unexpected to their players, wherever they choose to play. It’s where players can experience the anticipation of the unknown, and enjoy the buzz and excitement of playing big-name slots.

Project: Sky Vegas Millions

As part of a business need promote ‘that winning feeling’ the Sky Vegas Millions promotion was born. The promotion aimed to improve player perception of Sky Vegas and promote brand awareness. This would be done by offering tickets to players into a prize draw and giving away free prizes at set times.

A team was created to tackle this promotion which included myself representing UX/UI and front end implementation, Marketing, Brand Creative, Product, Compliance and Media.

Key meetings took place with stakeholders and the representatives of the brief team. In which it was decided that the promotion needed key prominence on the homepage and should be displayed in the hero banner and have a background takeover. It was also decided that they would like to have a dedicated detail page that could show a user’s progression towards their next ticket into the prize draw.

My role was to focus on the UX wireframes for desktop and the UI/Implementation across site. The main requirement for me was to highlight Sky Vegas Million on the homepage from a design point of view, and explore how to display complex ticket requirements to the user in an easy format.

Wireframes

Placement wireframes were created for desktop. Looking at the best way to let users know how many tickets they have and how close they are to winning their next ticket. Logged out we also needed to let user’s know how much was still left to win and when the next prize draw would be.

Applying the branding

The final Sky Vegas Millions logo was provided by brand and which meant wireframes could be visualised and fed back on. Homepage designs could also start to be designed and creating a real visual identity for Sky Vegas Millions could begin.

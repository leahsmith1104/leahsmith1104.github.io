Record Prints

The record prints are designed for music lovers. A way of remembering special memories, first gigs, favourite gigs or anniversaries. Each print is a personalised record with the lyrics of the chosen song by the artist wrapped around the middle to create the record itself. Each lyric line is broken by a circle before the next starts. The records contain the artist, song length and title. They can also be personalised with special dates.

Geometric Animals

Client: Riverfox is my own brand in which I carry out any work completed out of my full-time employment hours. It currently consists of work done for either my own personal gain, or at requests of friends/family. I aspire in future to possibly grow this brand and produce print work for a wider audience.

Design Thinking: The geometric animal prints are a set of woodland creatures. They have been built using illustrator. The colours have been picked to denote the real thing. Multiple shades have been either darkened to highlight shadows, or lightened to an extreme to highlight the opposite. They have been reflected so they are identical.

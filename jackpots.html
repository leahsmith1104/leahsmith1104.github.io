Jackpot Content

Client: Sky Betting and Gaming - Sky Vegas the is the UK's most popular online casino, it's the ultimate destination for new, exclusive, and exciting online slots. They bring the thrill of the unexpected to their players, wherever they choose to play. It’s where players can experience the anticipation of the unknown, and enjoy the buzz and excitement of playing big-name slots.

Project: Jackpot content (Jackpot King and Party Pots).

These are dedicated sections on the homepage to promote specific game content, which are tied together via jackpots. It came from a business need to promote jackpot games as exclusive content and a reason to get customers to continue playing with Sky Vegas. This was a cross product project including Product, Technology, Marketing and myself looking at the design and front end implementation.

Design Thinking: Jackpot King

The focus of the project was to incorporate the Jackpot king branding with game content to create a fun and exciting section that stands out from other content to attract the user’s attention. To do this I looked at centralising the brand of jackpot king and making sure the largest jackpot sum was visually the biggest, central and most prominent alongside the two smaller sums. A different font colour was used to make the jackpot amounts stand out from the titles and explanatory text. Finally, to make the section stand out from the normal content above and below, I included the jackpot king brand background and also a tapered border top and bottom. On mobile the background was lost as the site sits on a white, not dark background. Testing proved that a strip banner halted people scrolling any further down the page, which was not an objective.

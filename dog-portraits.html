Dog Portraits

Client: Riverfox is my own brand in which I carry out any work completed out of my full-time employment hours. It currently consists of work done for either my own personal gain, or at requests of friends/family. I aspire in future to possibly grow this brand and produce print work for a wider audience.

The dog portrait prints as made freehand using illustrator. They are made by layering many freehand drawn shapes on top of each other to develop depth and colour variation seen in the original photos. When finished they can be turned into mixed typography print pieces to make a special personalised present or they can be left alone as a piece of art and purely framed.

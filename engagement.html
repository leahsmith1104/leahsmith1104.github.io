Engagement Print

This print piece is a special personalised piece done for a couple who got engaged at an elephant sanctuary in Bali. The actual ring is hand drawn in illustrator, to match the engagement ring used in the proposal. The elephants are also hand drawn in illustrator and are there to support the main focal point of the ring and also act as a reminder of the event itself and the location.
